Vehicle And Asset Tracking

Made Effective And Easy

The Amber Connect Vehicle Tracking System is the first in the industry to employ artificial intelligence. The state-of-the-art system provides real-time GPS location data, generates instantaneous anti-theft alerts, and can remotely and automatically shut down the engine in case of unauthorized use. Our mission is to provide safety, security and peace of mind to our customers, provide business intelligence for commercial verticals and create a safer and a more secure society by offering affordable IoT security solutions.

Explore Your Possibilities

Amber Shield

Amber Shield allows your vehicle to fight back! This is an artificially Intelligent feature which automatically shuts down the engine and alerts you on the app when the vehicle detects unauthorized use.

Live Tracking And Monitoring

Real-time tracking (1 second refresh) with current speed, journey distance and time. Switch between map view and satellite view with ignition status.

Intelligent Insights

Get detailed statistics of travel history, with a range of real-time alerts such as geo-fencing, device tamper, SOS panic button and more.

Cost Management Tools

With knowledge of your fuel cost, idle-time alerts and calculations for every trip, and smart driving insights, our users benefit from increased cost-efficiency and savings.

Miniature But Powerful Devices

Our devices are discreet and can be easily hidden in your car, boat, a bag or on your person. We leverage GNSS and GSM technology to collect location points worldwide so you don't have to worry about your devices losing coverage. Our devices give real-time driving feedback and alerts to friends and family if and when there's an emergency.
